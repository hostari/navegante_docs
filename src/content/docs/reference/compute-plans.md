## What are Compute Plans?

Compute Plans are subscription tiers that define the computational resources allocated to your [Infrastructure Project](../infrastructure-projects). You select a Compute Plan when creating a new Infrastructure Project. All Navegante accounts are entitled to 1 free Infrastructure Project on the Hobby Plan.

Navegante uses flat-rate pricing, not usage-based billing. You're free to maximize your plan's resources as long as your workloads fit within the computational limits of your selected tier.

## Available Plans

| Plan | Price | CPU | RAM | Storage | Billing |
|------|-------|-----|-----|---------|---------|
| **Hobby** | $0/mo | 1vCPU | 2GB | 10GB SSD | Free forever |
| **Hobby Plus** | $15/mo | 2vCPU | 4GB | 30GB SSD | Paid Monthly |
| **Pro** | $25/mo | 4vCPU | 8GB | 70GB SSD | Paid Monthly |
| **Enterprise** | $150/mo | 24vCPU | 128GB | 2TB SSD | Paid Yearly |

### Hobby Plan

**$0/mo** — Free forever

- 1vCPU / 2GB RAM / 10GB SSD
- Virtual Machine
- Deploy unlimited repositories within resource limits
- Perfect for static sites and lightweight apps with minimal compute needs

### Hobby Plus Plan

**$15/mo** — Paid Monthly

- 2vCPU / 4GB RAM / 30GB SSD
- Virtual Machine
- Deploy unlimited repositories within resource limits
- Ideal for small web apps and APIs that need moderate compute power

### Pro Plan

**$25/mo** — Paid Monthly

- 4vCPU / 8GB RAM / 70GB SSD
- Virtual Machine
- Deploy unlimited repositories within resource limits
- Built for production apps with databases and higher traffic demands

### Enterprise Plan

**$150/mo** — Paid Yearly

- 24vCPU / 128GB RAM / 2TB SSD (Bare Metal)
- Everything from Pro
- Dedicated machine, not shared with other Navegante customers
- Dedicated resources for enterprise applications with intensive compute requirements
